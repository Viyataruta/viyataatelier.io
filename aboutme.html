Hi! I'm Viyata, a data visualization student at Purdue University's College of Polytechnic, passionate about transforming complex data into engaging, interactive stories. Originally from Cupertino, CA, I blend data analytics, UI/UX design, and visual storytelling to create intuitive and compelling visuals.

My expertise includes interactive dashboards, front-end development, and design principles like typography, branding, and user experience research. I work with Python, JavaScript, Tableau, and Adobe Suite to bring data to life.

Beyond academics, I'm Taylor Swift-obsessed, a former Starbucks barista, and a creative at heart—whether designing, baking, or building my project, BakeSpot. Let's connect and collaborate!
